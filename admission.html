Admission (Junior High)

Requirements

    Form 138 (Elementary Card)
    Original and Photocopy of Birth Certificate
    Three (3) copies of recent 2x2 picture
    Original and Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate
    Good Moral Certificate

Procedure

    Go to designated spots for registration
    Go to the Admissions and Registration Office for the submission of requirements and approval.
    Pay up down payment at Payment Office.
    Present receipt at the registrar office.
