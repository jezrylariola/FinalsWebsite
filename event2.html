Columban's Annual Buwan ng Wika Celebration

Columban College celebrate "Buwan ng Wika" as accordance to the declaration of the government. It celebrates the creation and the declaration of the national language, which is Filipino. The Filipino department organize this event every year as a way of gving importance to this time.
The event includes cultural dances and cultural attires competition. Each program department and section get a chance to compete and show they're indigenous inspired outfit.
One of the main event of the entire celebration is the "Mr. and Ms. Buwan ng Wika". The competing students are called Lakan for males and Lakambini for females. The winner will be decided through a voting system.
