Columban's Annual Sportsfest

Columban College holds an annual sport intramurals every September or October called "Palarong Columban". It is held to discover and enhance the skills and talents of student athletes and also wannabee varsity players.
The event includes numerous sports like basketball, volleyball, swimming, chess, table tennis, badminton, futsal, and many others
The event also includes a cheerleading competition and also the annual Mr. and Ms. Palarong Columban.
